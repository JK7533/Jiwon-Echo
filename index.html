LA Volume Index (2015 ASE)

  • Normal: ≤34 cm²/m²
  • Mild: 35–41
  • Moderate: 42–48
  • Severe: >48

Aortic Measurement

  • Use diastole, leading-edge, perpendicular to flow
  • Aortic Root: Z-score >2 = enlarged; 2–4.4 cm: mild, 4.5–4.9: mod, ≥5.0: severe (independent of BSA)
  • Arch: ≥3.0 cm = enlarged
  • Desc. Aorta: ≥3.0: mild, ≥4.0: mod, ≥5.0: severe
  • Upper Abd. Aorta: ≥3.0: mild, ≥4.0: mod, ≥5.0: severe
